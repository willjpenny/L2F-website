What we do

Free, specialist-backed resources for New Zealand families

Listening to Families is a free online resource designed to help New Zealand parents and caregivers navigate child and adolescent health. Our library covers 31 topics in child development, mental health, and wellbeing — from ADHD and Autism to trauma, school attendance, and medication.

Every topic is presented through videos and podcasts recorded by experienced New Zealand specialists. These are the conversations families often wish they could have had in the clinic — explained clearly, without jargon, and free to access at any time.

Our story

Where it started

Listening to Families began with a simple observation: the information that specialists share with families during clinical appointments is incredibly valuable, yet rarely accessible beyond those walls. Parents leave appointments with questions they didn't get to ask. Caregivers search the internet and find conflicting or poorly sourced advice.

We set out to change that by bringing New Zealand's specialist knowledge directly to families, in a format they can return to and share with others.

How it works

Straight from the specialists

Each topic in our library is presented by one or more New Zealand health specialists who work directly with children and families. Topics are organised by theme and presented in short video and podcast formats that can be watched or listened to at home, in your own time.

We also provide downloadable PDF summaries covering the key points from each topic, so families can save and share information easily.

Content is reviewed regularly to make sure it reflects current best practice. All resources are free to access and share.

Funding

Supported by Māia Health Foundation

Listening to Families is funded by Māia Health Foundation, which supports health initiatives that make a real difference for New Zealand families. Their backing makes it possible for us to keep all resources free and to continue growing our library.

Māia Health Foundation

Māia Health Foundation invests in projects that improve the health and wellbeing of children, young people, and families across New Zealand. Their support enables Listening to Families to remain free and to reach the families who need it most.
