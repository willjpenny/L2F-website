Listening to Families: Design options

Video watched indicator

Two options for showing users which videos they've already watched. Shown in context as a list of video cards, with a mix of watched and unwatched states.

Option A

Progress bar

A thin coloured bar runs along the bottom edge of the thumbnail. Full bar = fully watched. This approach also supports showing partial progress (e.g. a half-watched video).

Option B

Watched text label

A small "Watched" label with a tick appears in the card content area. Low visual noise and keeps the thumbnail area clean. Relies on text rather than iconography alone.
